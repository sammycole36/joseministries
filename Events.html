Aims and Objectives
of the Youth/Student Ministry

To serve the LORD in one spirit and promoting the work of God. To put in the youth the act of self-discipline, selfless service and social justice in a bid to encourage the youth to live an exemplary and God-fearing life. To propagate the gospel of our LORD Jesus Christ through evangelism.

JOSEM is a Family of Faith that is committed to Bible teaching and joyful worship. Children, youth and adults of all ages are ministered to and given opportunities to serve the Lord by serving others. You are invited to join us and experience what it is like to be a part of the family. We have much to offer you. Rise is a state Church where each person is made to feel welcomed and wanted. We take seriously our mission statement, which is "To Know Jesus and make Him known; To Follow the Leadership of the Holy Spirit; and to Take God's Word to God's World."
